$h(x,y) := \dfrac{-x}{x^2+y^2}$
\begin{align*}
    h(\alpha x,\alpha y) &= \dfrac{-\alpha x}{(\alpha x)^2+(\alpha y)^2} \\
    &= \dfrac{-x}{\alpha x^2+\alpha y^2} \\
    &= \dfrac{-x}{\alpha (x^2 + y^2)}  \\
    &= \alpha^{-1} h(x,y)
\end{align*}
Therefore $h$ is a homogeneous function of degree -1.
